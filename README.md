# E'rea RP Player

A personal RP item bag for Turtle WoW roleplay events.

## What it does
- Gives you a 16-slot bag dedicated to RP quest items, separate from your regular inventory
- Lets you receive, trade, show, and read items during RP events
- Items are managed by Game Masters running the **E'rea RP Master** addon

## Commands
- `/rpplayer` — open your RP bag
- `/rpplayer reset` — reset window positions if they go off-screen

## Notes
- This addon is required for participation in E'rea RP events
- Items only exist within this addon — they are not real in-game items
